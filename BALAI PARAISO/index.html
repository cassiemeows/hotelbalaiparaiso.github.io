THE MAN BEHIND
BALAI PARAISO

     Balai Paraiso grew out of one man - Don Alvar - who defined Asian hospitality and set new standards on luxury and what it means to be Filipino. It was built originally for his deceased wife Donya Isabella who was an environmentalist. Years later, it was fully developed as an eco-luxury resort in General Santos City. Our founder Don Alvar's belief rests on the principle that as owners, it is our duty to look after our staff well, to treat everyone kindly and generously. We owe our success to the dedication and commitment of our people. Their authenticity and passion to serve our customers and to relate on a personal level have made us an undisputed leader in Asian hospitality.

ALL-INCLUSIVE PROGRAMS

Mental Health
Retreat

     Balai Paraiso's Mental Health programs consist of a series of services tailored to one's individual needs with this goal in mind: bring you to a space of genuine mental well-being.

Yoga Sessions

     Yoga not only disciplines the body but unfolds the infinite potentials of the human mind and soul. Learn the foundations of yoga and ignite your path towards holistic wellness and healing. At Balai Paraiso, you may incorporate various types of yoga and Guided Medication into your regular practice to help keep your mind, body and spirit well-balanced.

Nature Walking

     An energizing way to finish your daily movement activities! Join our guided walk on 4.7km to 6km power walk in and around Balai Paraiso (Weather Permitting).

Functional Training

     Functional Fitness involves training the body for the activities performed in everyday life.

Sound Healing

     Sound therapy is a clinical and evidence-based therapy used for dealing mental health issues, such as anxiety, pain, and sleep disorders. These issues are usually caused by high levels of tension and stress. Sound Healing brings one to a relaxed state with sounds created by singing bowls, tuning forks, and other resonant musical instruments, including the human voice.

Interactive Wellness
Workshops

     Art Therapy - Experience how art, through therapeutic form drawing or working with colors helps establish brain pathways to healing senile dementia, symbols related to the heart and breathing issues and hormonal imbalance.

Organic Farm
Tour

     Balai Paraiso's 5700 sqm. organic vegetable & herb garden is an example of organic precision agriculture. Everything grown on the land has been designed to fit the resort's culinary needs. Balai Paraiso is designed to have seasonal rotational crops for true sustainable, organic farm-to-table experience.

Wellness Cuisine

     Balai Paraiso's wellness cuisine-vegan, vegetarian, and pescetarian are essential components of the healing food concept which advocates plant-based, locally, sourced, organically grown, and farm-to-table philosophy.
